University Admissions

Using our familiarity and successful track record with the college admissions process, we have selected universities and edited application essays since 2015. Since 2018 we have also edited Statements of Purpose (SOP) for Ph.D. and Masters applicants.

Our team of Caltech graduates helps high schoolers and undergraduates choose universities, craft admissions strategies, edit essays, and estimate probabilities of admission. Our editors also happen to be recent successful applicants themselves.

Frequently asked questions

Q: What is "Goopa"?

A: "Goopa" is the correct way to read "GPA" (Grade Point Average)

Ex: "The marginal contribution of individual courses on Goopa decreases in magnitude as the number of total courses increases."

Inquiries

Please include the following information when reaching out by WeChat (user: eonsin) or email (eonsin[αt]126[。]com):

  1. Are you applying for undergraduate, master's, or Ph.D. admissions?

  2. Which year are you currently in?

  3. Are there any specific schools you would like to apply to, and for what general area of study? Do you have any specific career plans after graduation? These are perfectly OK to not know.

  4. What are some activities or awards you plan to write about or list in your application? The application has limited space, and our editors are very good at picking the important activities and awards, so please be as thorough as possible.

  5. You agree that your information may be shared among our editors for planning, strategy, and editing purposes. We will not share your information with other applicants. If we proceed with editing and you are admitted to any schools, your initials, not your full name, may be posted to the above chart.
